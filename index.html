MOODLE

 PROBLEM

Many indivduals struggle to seek help for mental health as it is so stigmatized. However, it must be known that mental health is more important than it may seem.

  GOAL

As a group, we planned to educate young teens early in life to bring awareness of mental health and to disstigmatize the topic more.

  ROLE

In this team-based project, my role was to work on the UX/UI elements regarding app layout, colour, interactivity.

CAMPUS CHAOS

 PROBLEM

Create a Javascript P5 game that contains elements learned from CS105.

  GOAL

I wanted to create a game that portrays the geese population at the University of Waterloo. I thought it was a funny and harmless way to display a popular characteristic of University of Waterloo, while still being creative and humorous.

  ROLE

Since this was a solo project, my job was to create a game characters and items, game score board, game mechanics, and finally execute this using javascript p5.

INTERGALACTIC HEIST

 PROBLEM

In modern society there is a constant trend for materialistic items due to capitalism. As kids follow this trend, it has been more difficult for them to make genuine connects with friends and family.

  GOAL

I wanted to create a game for pre-teens that displays the insignificance of materialism in comparison to friendship and companionship.

  ROLE

Since this was a solo project, my job was to write a storyline, create alterate role play paths, and finally execute this using HTML and Javascript.
